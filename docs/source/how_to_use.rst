How To Use
===========

This guide covers all the features and functionality of GrowKitty, helping you make the most of your habit-building journey.

Dashboard Overview
------------------

The main dashboard is your command center for habit tracking:

**Layout Components**

1. **Virtual Cat Display** (Center)
   
   - Shows your cat's current state and mood
   - Reacts to your habit completion rate
   - Displays growth stages and accessories

2. **Habit List** (Left Panel)
   
   - All your active habits
   - Quick check-off buttons
   - Streak indicators
   - Success rate percentage

3. **Mission Board** (Right Panel)
   
   - Today's random missions
   - Reward information
   - Completion status
   - Timer until new missions

4. **Statistics Bar** (Top)
   
   - Overall success rate
   - Current streak
   - Total habits completed
   - Level and experience points

Managing Habits
---------------

Creating Habits
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

To add a new habit:

1. Click **"+ Add Habit"** button
2. Fill in the habit details:

   .. code-block:: text

      Name: Morning Exercise
      Category: Health & Fitness
      Frequency: Daily
      Time: 07:00 AM
      Reminder: Enabled
      Notes: 30 minutes of cardio

3. Click **"Save Habit"**

**Best Practices**

- Start with 3-5 habits to avoid overwhelm
- Be specific (e.g., "Run 3km" vs "Exercise")
- Choose realistic frequencies
- Set reminders for new habits

Editing Habits
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

To modify an existing habit:

1. Click the habit card
2. Select **"Edit"** icon (‚úèÔ∏è)
3. Update fields as needed
4. Click **"Save Changes"**

You can change:

- Habit name and description
- Category and tags
- Frequency and timing
- Reminder settings

Completing Habits
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

To mark a habit as complete:

**Quick Complete**

- Click the checkbox next to the habit name
- The habit card will animate with confirmation
- Your cat will react positively

**Detailed Logging**

1. Click the habit card
2. Add notes about today's completion (optional)
3. Rate your performance (1-5 stars)
4. Click **"Mark Complete"**

Skipping Habits
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

If you need to skip a habit:

1. Click the habit card
2. Select **"Skip Today"**
3. Optionally add a reason
4. Click **"Confirm Skip"**

.. note::
   Skipping too many days in a row will affect your cat's mood and growth rate.

Mission System
--------------

Understanding Missions
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Missions are daily challenges that:

- Add variety to your routine
- Prevent monotony and boredom
- Provide bonus rewards
- Unlock special cat accessories

Mission Types
~~~~~~~~~~~~~

**Daily Missions** (3 per day)

1. **Habit Combination**: Complete 3 specific habits today
2. **Perfect Day**: Complete all habits without skipping
3. **Early Bird**: Complete 2 habits before 10 AM
4. **Consistency**: Maintain your streak for 7 days
5. **Explorer**: Try a new habit category

**Weekly Challenges**

- Complete 50 total habits this week
- Achieve 90%+ success rate
- Share progress with 3 friends
- Unlock 5 new cat accessories

**Special Events**

- Seasonal missions (holidays, seasons)
- Community events
- Limited-time challenges

Completing Missions
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Missions auto-complete when requirements are met:

1. Check mission progress on the Mission Board
2. Complete the required habits/actions
3. Receive instant notification and rewards
4. See your cat's special reaction

Mission Rewards
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Rewards are automatically added:

- **Experience Points**: 50-200 XP per mission
- **Coins**: 10-50 coins for shop purchases
- **Accessories**: Exclusive cat items
- **Badges**: Achievement unlocks

Tips for Success
----------------

Best Practices
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

**Starting Out**

- Begin with 3-5 manageable habits
- Set realistic goals based on current routine
- Enable reminders for new habits
- Review progress daily

**Building Momentum**

- Celebrate small wins
- Focus on consistency over perfection
- Use streak freezes strategically
- Adjust habits as needed

**Long-term Success**

- Regularly review and update habit list
- Challenge yourself with new categories
- Engage with community features
- Track trends to identify patterns

Common Pitfalls
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

**Avoid These Mistakes**

- Adding too many habits at once
- Setting unrealistic expectations
- Ignoring your cat's feedback
- Skipping without reason
- Not celebrating achievements

**Recovery Strategies**

- Use a streak freeze if needed
- Adjust habit difficulty
- Take a rest day mindfully
- Re-engage with easier habits
- Remember progress isn't linear
